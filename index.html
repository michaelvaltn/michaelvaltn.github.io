Management Consulting

Provide expert advise to organizations on a specific project or more broadly on the structure and practices of a firm to improve their performance by solving their issues and encourage growth. The work applied to every industries with a wide range of issues, thus the consultants must constantly learn and adapt.

Why I'm interested in it?

Management consulting is a path that I'm very interested in for several reasons. I personally feel that I am a generalist, I like to learn how many things work in the world, an as an Accounting student I'm familliar with business processes, so I think management consulting fits me on that.

Technology

A sum of techniques, skills, methods, and processes used in production of goods and services or in the accomplishment of objectives, such as investigation, research, etc. In technology consulting, it is to advise clients of how to use their IT practices to achieve their business objectives.

Why I'm interested in it?

21st century is hot with technology and digital transformation. Thus I have to allow myself to be literate in digital and technology since I will be using it a lot. Technology consulting also pique my interest since it allows me to learn a lot about tech and makes me more relevant in this digital age.

Auditing

An independent examination to check whether it follows the requirements, free of fraud, and ensure the true and fairness of the process or system (but usually associated with financial information). Auditing is beneficial to stakeholders that they acquired assurance of the information.

Why I'm interested in it?

Auditing is directly aligned with my degree and it is one of my favorite subject in Accounting program. I really enjoy the subject and it is very interesting for me, as it is very relevant in the business world and perhaps one of the most important services that is needed by companies.

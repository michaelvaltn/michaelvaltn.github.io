Auditor

A professional who executes audits on the client's company.

They are the professionals certified to audits financial information or information systems (depending on their specialty) in order to provide assurances to the stakeholders involved with the company. Their tasks range from gathering evidence, test management assertion against criteria, and determines the overall fairness of the company's financial statement.

Subjects I learn as an Accounting Student

Warren Buffet once said that Accounting is the language of business. It teaches you about how to measure, process, and communicate all sorts of financial information and also allows you to understand the process of a business. Some subjects that I learn includes:

Financial Accounting

A field of Accounting that is concerned with summary, analysis, and report of all transaction that happened in the company. I learn about how to properly record transaction and how to prepare financial report in accordance to standard Accounting rules like IFRS or Indonesia's PSAK.

Financial Auditing

A systematic examination of accounts and documents of the company to ensure the true and fairness of the information. I learn a lot about the audit risk, audit engagement, audit tests (risk assestment, test of control, and substantive procedure), and procedures or ways to gather audit evidences.

Accounting Information System

The system of collecting, storing and processing accounting information. This subject teaches me a lot about various business processes and the ideal & proper control that must be imposed on the process to reduce the risk of fraud.

Cost Accounting

A field of accounting concerned with recording and processing measurements of cost in manufacturing company. I learn a lot about methods used to measure cost and the process behind calculating Cost of Goods Sold.
